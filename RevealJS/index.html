Problem Summary

With 4 plants, 50 customers, and 5 products to deliver to each customers throughout the US:

  1. Find the minimum number of warehouses to achieve 80% coverage of customers within 500 miles
  2. Find optimal allocation of resources that maximizes profit with an option to upgrade plants

Scenario 1 Solution

Recommendation: Place warehouses in Customer locations 5, 8, 31, 39 to achieve the 500-mile coverage below:

Product 1: 84.8%
Product 2: 80.94%
Product 3: 90.1%
Product 4: 87.35%
Product 5: 92.01%

Transportation Cost: $108.26M (+$9.32M from baseline which is $98.94M)
